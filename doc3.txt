— Eto ya, dobryy E-ekh, ya zdes'.
— I ya zdes'!
— A ty kto takoy? Otkuda vzyalsya?
— S togo berega morya.
— Na chyom priyekhal?
— Osedlal khromuyu blokhu, sel i priyekhal.
— More chto — luzha?
— Mozhet, i luzha, da tol'ko tu luzhu oryol ne pereletel.
— Znachit, oryol — ptenets?
— Navernoye, ptenets, no ten' ot yego kryl'yev gorod zakryvayet, v gorode noch' nastayot.
— Gorod, nebos' — krokhotnyy?
— Cherez tot gorod zayats begal — ne perebezhal.
— Vykhodit, zayats — malen'kiy?
— Zayats kak zayats, iz yego shkury tulup vyshel.
— Kuda vyshel?
— Vyshel iz togo goroda, gde zayats begal, na kotoryy ten' ot orla upala, i poshyol kuda glyadyat.
— Ch'i glaza?
— Glaza togo tulupa, kotoryy iz shkury zaytsa vyshel, v gorode, gde noch' nastayot, kogda nad nim ptenets proletayet verkhom na khromoy blokhe.
— Chego-o?!
— Chego-chego, na khromoy blokhe s togo berega morya, kotoroye zaytsu ne pereletet', orlu ne perebezhat', khot' more — ne more, a tak, luzha posredi goroda, gde ten' ot blokhi na zaytsa upala i nasmert' ubila, a iz shkury zaytsa tulup vyshel i poshyol kuda glyadyat, a tut zayats ka-ak prygnet!
— Kakoy zayats?!
— Nasmert' ubityy, kak prygnet kuda glyadyat — azh na tot bereg morya, kotoroye ni pereletet', ni perebezhat', iz kotorogo tulup vyshel, na kotoryy ten' upala i zaytsa ubila, khot' zayats — ne zayats, a oryol!
— Kakoy zayats?! Kakoy oryol?!! Kakaya blokha-a-a-a-a-a-a?!!!
— Povtorit'? Nu, znachit, ta samaya blokha s togo berega luzhi…
— A-a-a-a-a, da khva-ati-i-i-it!!!
— Ey, pogodi, daleko yeshche do rassveta!
